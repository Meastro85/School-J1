# The Physical Connection

Physical connection must be established.
Can be:
- Wireless
- Wired

A [[NIC]] connects a device to a network. 
Some devices have just one, others have multiple.
Not all connections offer the same level of performance

# The Physical Layer

Transports bits across the media.
Accepts complete frames from the [[Datalink]] Layer.
Encodes it as a series of signals.
Last step in encapsulation process.
